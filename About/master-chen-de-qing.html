Master Chen De Qing

??

Master Chen is a genuine 5th level master from Kaifeng, Henan Province, Mainland China. He has won five gold medals in national sports university wushu competitions in his homeland.

This is a rare opportunity to train directly under someone with a very high level in genuine Chinese Martial Arts. He has been training since the age of 8, first studying at the legendary Shaolin Temple in Henan Province, then going on to both train and teach at some of the best martial arts centres in China.

Since arriving in Manchester in late 1999, Master Chen has rapidly established a reputation as a quality martial arts teacher and performer and is in increasing demand for his exciting wushu and taichi demonstrations. Check out the video section of this site to see Master Chen in action.

Other Activities

Master Chen and his students are often asked to participate in various events from promotional to corporate, and cultural events throughout the year.

Students from Jin Long have been attending the UK National Wushu Championships since 2002, and achieving great results in both Junior and Adult levels. Jin Long also has students who are the representatives of the British Wushu team, who take part in European and World wushu championships.

Master Chen also visits different schools and societies around the country for seminars and performances. He has been regularly invited to do seminars at Imperial College in London.
