Arts & Crafts

艺术&手工艺

Curating unique and characteristic Chinese arts and crafts to promote their heritage in the UK.

Jinlong has been actively promoting Chinese Arts & Crafts in the UK bringing over some extraordinary artists to display and demonstrate their works and skills. The artists we work with are mostly National Intangible Cultural Heritage inheritors practising the artistry which are UNESCO enlisted.
